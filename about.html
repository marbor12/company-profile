About idSpora

For over one year, we've provided innovative learning experiences to support personal and professional growth.

Our Story

IdSpora originated from a research initiative conducted by a collaborative alliance known as Ko+Lab. The research highlighted the urgent need for a platform that could bridge the gap between student potential and the innovative ideas of lecturers in the fields of education, technology, and human resource development.

Based on these findings, Ko+Lab took the initiative to establish a startup that facilitates the development of talents, interests, and creative ideas through event-based and digital platform approaches. IdSpora officially began operating in November 2024, founded by a group of students and academics with backgrounds in technology and education. Its early activities started within a small community actively organizing training sessions, seminars, online sharing events, and focus group discussions both on and off campus.

IdSpora is categorized as a digital startup operating in the field of educational services and youth development platforms. It is currently in the early growth stage, focusing on market validation, core feature development, and expanding its partner network.
